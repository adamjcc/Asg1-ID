ABOUT THE BAND

One Direction (1D) was a globally famous English-Irish pop boy band formed on The X Factor in 2010, featuring members Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Louis Tomlinson, Liam Payne, and Zayn Malik (who left in 2015). Known for hit albums like Up All Night and Midnight Memories, they achieved massive success with catchy pop-rock songs, five albums, and world tours before going on an indefinite hiatus in 2016 to pursue solo careers, with all members finding success in music and other fields.
